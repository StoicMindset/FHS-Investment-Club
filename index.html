Who we are

The FHS Investment Club is a student-led club at Franklin High School in Franklin, TN. The Club was founded in 2022 by Caedmon Holland and Jason Johnson. It is a chapter of Young Investors Society. The teacher sponsor for FHSIC is Mr. Watson. The club meets in room 706 every Monday from 3:00-4:00 pm.
